Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of cricket.

Career Highlights

  • World Cup Winning Captain - 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup
  • Five-time IPL Champion with Chennai Super Kings
  • First captain to win all three ICC trophies (T20 World Cup, ODI World Cup, Champions Trophy)
  • Over 10,000 runs in ODI cricket
  • Best finisher in limited-overs cricket
